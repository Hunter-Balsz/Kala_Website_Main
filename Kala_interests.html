A few of Kala's interests

  • Kala loves to play with her toys, one of her favorite things to do when playing is chase a laser pointer like a cat! Even if we say the word laser she starts to get excited and will look on the ground for the little red dot!
  • Another thing Kala loves is exploring, this could include walks, areas of the house after moving around furniture, running around outside and at our property! When she is exploring at our property she will sometimes dissapear for hours, it worries me sometimes but she always makes it back! I recently got certified to fly drones so next time we go camping I'm going to watch her explore from the air!
  • The last thing Kala likes doing that I'm going to cover is car rides! She is like every other dog and loves them, she does great in the car and as soon as a window gets rolled down even a little, she sticks her nose right out it!
  • Kala also loves going to the beach! We haven't been for a few years but it's one of her favorite places to go! Whenever we go she love to hop around in the water and then roll around in the sand, it's often quite the mess to clean up. At the end of the day though it's always worth it, she is always so happy to go and smell all the new fun stuff while playing in the water and sand!
  • One thing that Kala loves even more than the beach is the snow! It's really funny watching her move through it when it's a couple feet high becasue she'll jump her way through the snow, stop every onece in awhile to take a look around, and then continuing to jump to her destination. Another thing she really likes to do in the snow is to open her mouth and shovel the snow through it. She never eats it, just gets large amounts to run through her mouth. I've never understood why but it's always quite funny! The last thing she really likes about the snow is bits of ice, she's one of the dogs that likes to chew on it. The snow is one of her favorite times of the year and it's really unfortunate it doesn't last longer in Washington.
